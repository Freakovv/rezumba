## Overview

Built and maintain the production website for **Libriform — logistics company**, a full-stack web platform with service landing pages, lead tracking, and mobile distribution.

## What I did

- Designed and developed the company website from scratch
- Deployed to production on a **VPS** with **Docker**, **nginx**, and **CI/CD** via Git
- Built an **Android app** wrapper with Capacitor
- Implemented **SEO**: meta tags, sitemap, schema markup, and dedicated service landing pages
- Integrated **Yandex.Metrica** and lead tracking analytics
- Manage **Yandex Direct ad campaigns:** setup, optimization, and lead performance tracking

## Stack

Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, MySQL, Docker, nginx, VPS, Git, CI/CD, Capacitor (Android)

## Results

A production-ready platform that handles real business traffic, captures leads, and supports ongoing marketing campaigns.
